PSL-F138-20 - 13.8V/20A Linear Power Supply

Development Status: This kit is currently under development and expected to be available in Q2 2026.

The PSL-F138-20 is a robust linear power supply designed for amateur radio operators who demand the cleanest possible power for their equipment. Based on the proven design from Ludens.cl, this kit delivers exceptional performance with minimal noise and ripple.

Key Specifications

  • Output Voltage: 13.8V
  • Continuous Current: 20A
  • Current Limiting: Approximately 25A

Innovative Design Features

Unconventional Architecture

The PSL-F138-20 uses an innovative approach where pass transistors are positioned on the negative rail in common-emitter configuration, rather than the typical emitter-follower arrangement. This design choice delivers exceptionally low voltage drop and improved efficiency.

Superior Heat Dissipation

Since collector connections attach directly to the negative output (typically grounded), no insulation is required between transistors and the cabinet. This significantly improves heat dissipation efficiency and simplifies construction.

Fail-Safe Voltage Adjustment

The voltage adjustment potentiometer is arranged so that if the wiper contact fails, the voltage will decrease rather than increase. This critical safety feature protects your valuable radio equipment from damage due to overvoltage conditions.

Cost-Effective Components

The design uses low-cost 25V filter capacitors rather than expensive 35V alternatives, reducing both cost and physical size without compromising performance.

Power Dissipation

At 20A continuous operation, the power supply dissipates approximately 200W in normal use. During short circuit conditions, pass transistors may reach 130W. A reasonably oversized heatsink is essential for safe operation at rated current levels.

Ideal Applications

  • HF and VHF/UHF transceivers requiring clean power
  • Sensitive receiver systems
  • QRP and homebrew radio projects
  • Amateur radio stations where noise-free operation is critical
  • Fixed 13.8v output voltage

What's Included

The PSL-F138-20 kit includes all necessary components, PCB, and assembly documentation. Available in both complete disassembled form or partially assembled configurations to suit your building preferences.
